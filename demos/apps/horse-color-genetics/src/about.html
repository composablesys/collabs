Collabs Horse Color Genetics Demo

A collaborative app where you edit a horse's genes and observe the effects on its coat color.

This is a collaborative version of an app by Jennifer Hoffman. Original app: http://horse.jenniferhoffman.net/horse-color-genetics.html. Since the original app is CC-BY-NC-SA-3.0 licensed, so is this version.
