Collabs Shapes (Semidirect Product) Demo

A demo of the semidirect product of CRDTs for manipulating shapes. To the extent possible, group operations affect not just the shapes, but also concurrent operations on those shapes. E.g., if you rotate the group, while concurrently, someone else translates (drags) one shape, then the result will be as if the whole group was rotated after the translation - thus the shapes maintain their relative positions.
